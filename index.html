Jasmine Kent is a...

Jasmine holds a Masters in Industrial Design, with consulting experience across medical, softgoods, and hard goods design. She has been in Active Apparel Fashion Design for the past 5 years, and recently completed a UX/UI Design Bootcamp through the University of Minnesota.

Blue sky thinking is the foundation of how I operate as a designer. My background in different design industries allow me to cross pollinate references and create new ideas.

For 5 years I operated as the Community Strategist for the Hue Collective who hosted Hue Design Summit annually. A Summit that fostered community amongst black designers in Visual Design, Inudstrial Design, and UX/UI Design.

I am the mother of a 5-year old daughter who just started Kindergarten this year. I am a certified Full Spectrum Birth Doula that supports Black mamas.
